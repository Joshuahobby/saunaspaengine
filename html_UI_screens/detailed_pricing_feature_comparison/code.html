Frequently Asked Questions

Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?

Yes, you can change your plan at any time. If you upgrade, the new pricing is prorated. If you downgrade, your new rate starts at the next billing cycle.

What happens after my 14-day free trial?

At the end of your trial, you'll be prompted to select a plan and provide payment details. Your data will be preserved so you can pick up where you left off.
