The Challenge

Before implementing Sauna SPA Engine, Aurora Wellness Center faced significant bottlenecks during peak hours. Manual booking logs and paper-based health intake forms created long wait times, detracting from the premium guest experience. Managing staff schedules across multiple thermal zones was becoming increasingly complex and prone to human error.

The Solution

We deployed our comprehensive SaaS suite, focusing on automated digital check-ins and real-time resource allocation. Guests now receive digital intake forms 24 hours before arrival, which sync directly with the center's CRM. The staff dashboard provides a live 'Heat Map' of thermal zones, allowing for dynamic crowd management and personalized service.

The Results

Beyond the 70% increase in check-in speed, Aurora saw a dramatic uplift in upsell opportunities. The platform's smart recommendation engine suggested treatments based on guest history, contributing to a 25% revenue growth in the first quarter alone. Staff satisfaction scores improved as manual data entry was eliminated, allowing them to focus entirely on guest hospitality.

"The transition was seamless. We didn't just buy software; we gained a strategic partner that understands the nuances of luxury wellness." — Elena Vance, Director of Operations
